Week 1: Foundation Building

Day 1: Cardio (30 mins brisk walking/jogging) + Stretching (10 mins)

Day 2: Strength (Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps, Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps, Plank: 30 seconds x 3)

Day 3: Cardio (20 mins cycling or jogging intervals: 1 min fast, 2 mins slow)

Day 4: Rest/Light Yoga (10 mins stretching, focusing on flexibility)

Day 5: Core & Lower Body (Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps, Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg, Side Plank: 20 seconds per side x 3)

Day 6: Cardio (High-Intensity Interval Training: 20 mins of sprinting and walking mix)

Day 7: Active Recovery (Relaxing walk + 5 mins light stretching)

Week 2: Progression

Day 8: Strength (Increase reps: Push-ups 3 sets of 15, Squats 3 sets of 15, Plank: 40 seconds)

Day 9: Cardio (Incline walking or fast cycling, 30 mins)

Day 10: Core (Add variations: Bicycle crunches, Flutter kicks, Side plank dips—3 sets each)

Day 11: Rest or Recovery Yoga (Introduce balance poses like Warrior or Tree Pose)

Day 12: Full Body (Push-ups, Burpees, Jump squats—3 sets of 10 reps each)

Day 13: Cardio & Agility (Include side shuffles and jump rope intervals, 20 mins)

Day 14: Relaxing recovery walk (30 mins, include foam rolling if available)

Week 3: Increased Intensity

Day 15: Strength & Resistance (Add weights or resistance bands if available: 3 sets x 12 reps for major muscles)

Day 16: Cardio (Longer sessions—40 mins)

Day 17: Core (Increase plank time to 1 min, include Russian twists with weights)

Day 18: Yoga or Rest (Focus on improving range of motion in shoulders and hips)

Day 19: Lower Body (Step-ups, Bulgarian split squats, Calf raises)

Day 20: Cardio & Plyometrics (Jump lunges, Jump squats, Box jumps—30 mins total workout)

Day 21: Recovery walk/stretching (20 mins light activity)

Week 4: Peak Performance

Day 22: Upper Body (Pull-ups, Chin-ups, Bench press—focus on higher reps/weights)

Day 23: Cardio (HIIT: 45 seconds work, 15 seconds rest for 20 mins)

Day 24: Core (Include plank variations—shoulder taps, mountain climbers)

Day 25: Rest/Yoga (Hold challenging poses for longer durations)

Day 26: Strength (Divide into Upper, Lower, Core splits for targeted training)

Day 27: Cardio Endurance (Long runs, brisk walks, or cycling—40 mins)

Day 28: Active Recovery Stretching (10–15 mins)

Day 29: Full Body Circuit (Combine cardio and strength exercises for 30 mins)

Day 30: Rest & Reflect (Celebrate your progress and reset for new goals!)
