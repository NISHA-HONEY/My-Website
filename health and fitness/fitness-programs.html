Week 1-2: Foundational Phase

Monday

Warm-up: 10 minutes brisk walking or light jogging Strength Training: Squats – 3 sets of 12 reps Push-ups – 3 sets of 10-12 reps Plank – 3 sets of 20-30 seconds hold Cool-down: Stretching for 5 minutes

Tuesday

Cardio: 20-30 minutes cycling or brisk walking Core workout: Leg raises – 3 sets of 10 reps Bicycle crunches – 3 sets of 12 reps

Wednesday

Active rest: Light yoga or a long walk

Thursday

Warm-up: 5-10 minutes jumping jacks or jogging Strength Training: Lunges – 3 sets of 12 reps (each leg) Dumbbell or water-bottle rows – 3 sets of 10 reps Wall sit – 3 rounds of 30 seconds Cool-down: Stretching for 5 minutes

Friday

Cardio: 20-30 minutes of swimming or jogging

Saturday

Full-body workout: Burpees – 3 sets of 8-10 reps Mountain climbers – 3 sets of 12 reps Side planks – 3 sets of 20-30 seconds (each side)

Sunday

Rest or light recovery yoga

Week 3-4: Progressive Phase

Increase reps and duration for strength and cardio exercises. Add weights if you feel comfortable. Incorporate interval training for cardio, e.g., alternating 1 minute of jogging with 30 seconds of sprinting.
