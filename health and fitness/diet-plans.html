To create a balanced and healthy diet plan, focus on incorporating a variety of whole foods, including fruits,
vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats, while limiting processed foods, added sugars, and unhealthy fats.

Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan 1 :

Breakfast:

Oatmeal topped with fresh fruits (e.g., bananas, berries) and a drizzle of honey or chia seeds. A boiled egg or a handful of almonds. Green tea or a glass of low-fat milk.

Mid-Morning Snack:

A small apple or a handful of mixed nuts.

Lunch:

Grilled chicken or paneer with a side of quinoa or brown rice. Steamed vegetables (like broccoli, carrots, and beans) seasoned with olive oil. A small bowl of salad with greens, cucumbers, and cherry tomatoes dressed in lemon.

Evening Snack:

A cup of green tea with roasted chickpeas or carrot sticks with hummus.

Dinner:

Grilled fish or tofu with a side of sautéed spinach and sweet potatoes. A whole-wheat tortilla or small roti. A small serving of yogurt.

Non-Vegetarian Diet Plan 2:

Breakfast:

Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast A side of fresh fruits like apple or papaya

Mid-Morning Snack:

Boiled egg or chicken salad Fresh coconut water or herbal tea

Lunch:

Grilled chicken or fish curry with steamed rice or chapati Steamed broccoli and carrots A light yogurt-based drink like lassi

Afternoon Snack:

Hard-boiled eggs or lean chicken slices with hummus

Dinner:

Baked fish or chicken stir-fry with veggies A bowl of lentil soup for added nutrients Sweet potato mash or salad as a side

Vegetarian Diet plan 1

Breakfast:

Smoothie bowl made with blended bananas, spinach, and unsweetened almond milk, topped with granola. A slice of whole-grain toast with almond butter.

Mid-Morning Snack:

A handful of walnuts or a small bowl of roasted makhana (foxnuts).

Lunch:

Lentil (dal) soup with a serving of brown rice. Stir-fried veggies with spices and olive oil. Cucumber and carrot salad.

Evening Snack:

A slice of avocado on whole-grain bread or a fruit of your choice.

Dinner:

Vegetable stir-fry with tofu or paneer. A small serving of millets (e.g., ragi or bajra). A glass of buttermilk or herbal tea.

Vegetarian Diet Plan 2

Breakfast:

Oats or muesli with almond milk, topped with fresh fruits and nuts A boiled sweet potato or whole-grain toast with peanut butter

Mid-Morning Snack:

Fresh fruit salad or a smoothie made with yogurt and fruits A handful of roasted chickpeas or makhana (lotus seeds)

Lunch:

Spinach and lentil curry with brown rice or whole wheat roti A side of cucumber and tomato salad One cup of curd (plain yogurt)

Afternoon Snack:

A small bowl of sprouts salad or roasted mixed seeds

Dinner:

Grilled paneer or tofu with sautéed vegetables Quinoa or millet-based dish with herbs A bowl of clear vegetable soup
